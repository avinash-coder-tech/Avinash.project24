Music Platform Overview

This platform allows users to upload music, create and manage artist profiles, listen to various tracks, and share their musical creations with the world. It also provides music streaming and subscription-based services.

Key Features

  • Music upload and streaming
  • Artist profiles with bio, discography, and music galleries
  • Subscription and monetization options
  • Interactive search and recommendations
  • Music licensing and rights management

Technical Requirements

  • Backend: Node.js or Python (Django/Flask)
  • Frontend: React, Angular, or Vue.js
  • Database: MySQL, PostgreSQL, or MongoDB
  • Cloud Hosting: AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud
  • Authentication: OAuth 2.0 for secure login
  • Payment Gateway: Stripe or PayPal for subscriptions
